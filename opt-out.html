Opt-In and Opt-Out Guidelines

By submitting your phone number or email address on our site, you affirmatively opt in to receive communications from Spring Global Health. We maintain a record of your consent, including how and when it was provided, to comply with industry best practices and Vercel requirements.

You may opt out of non-emergency messaging at any time by replying STOP to SMS messages, using the unsubscribe link in emails, or submitting our Opt-Out Form. You may also contact support@springglobalhealth.com for assistance.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact privacy@springglobalhealth.com.

Please use this form to opt out of communications from Spring Global Health. Your request will be sent to our support team.
