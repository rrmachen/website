The Spring Solution

1) Meet people where they are

Start on SMS, phone call, or WhatsApp — no app installs required.

2) Support the daily routine

Short, friendly check-ins and reminders tailored to the individual care plan.

3) Close the loop with clinicians

Escalate when patterns suggest risk. Generate concise summaries for care teams.

4) Build social support networks

Connect Spring patients living in the same area to cultivate community trust and sustainability.
